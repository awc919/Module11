Macon County is located in the state of North Carolina. The population is approximately 37,014. Its county seat is Franklin. The Nantahala River runs through Macon County, flowing into the Little Tennessee River in Swain County. Wikipedia

Macon County, North Carolina, is known for its natural beauty, particularly the Nantahala National Forest, which offers outdoor activities like whitewater rafting and hiking, as well as for its rich mineral and gemstone deposits, drawing gem enthusiasts and rockhounds.
